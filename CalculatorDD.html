This is a simple calculator that can add, subtract, multiply, and divide one digit numbers from 0-9. developed by KatKrate.

Welcome to KatKrate Calculator. This is a program that allows you to perform basic operations on multi-digit numbers.
Left Numberpad: The left side of the calculator contains 10 numbers 0-9. Clicking on one of them sets the very top number (x) to the selected number.
Right Numberpad: Same as left side, however only affects the number right below x. (y)
x,+,-,/: clicking on these buttons will perform the listed operation on x and y, and the output will show at the very bottom of the canvas.
